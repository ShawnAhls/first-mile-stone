About the Band


Ah, the joy of humble beginnings…


Metallica, one of the biggest selling acts in American history, were
born on October 28, 1981 when drummer Lars Ulrich and guitar
player/singer James Hetfield got together via Ulrich’s LA Recycler
newspaper ad. Soon, the two had recruited Hetfield’s friend and
housemate Ron McGovney to play bass, Dave Mustaine to play lead
guitar, and taken the name Metallica after a suggestion from Bay Area
metal scene friend Ron Quintana.
